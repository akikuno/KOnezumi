2. CRISPR gRNA candidates


CRISPR gRNA candidates are sorted as high specificity (MIT) and high cutting efficiency (Doench/Fusi).

Recommend to use gRNA candidates with MIT > 50 and Doench/Fusi > 66%(55) as bold-highlighted below.

The details of each score are described at UCSC CRISPR/Cas9 track.

Guide sequence is linked to CRISPRdirect.


3. Primer candidates for genotyping


Recommend to select primers with higher Score and lower Penalty.

Score indicates primer specificity as described below.

  • Score 3: high-specific primers
    		•  : 1 site for 0 mismatch, < 20 sites for 1 mismatch, and < 100 sites for 2 mismatches
  • Score 2: mid-specific primers
    		•  : 1 site for 0 mismatch, < 20 sites for 1 mismatch, but > 100 sites for 2 mismatches
  • Score 1: low-specific primers
    		•  : 1 site for 0 mismatch, but > 20 sites for 1 mismatch, > 100 sites for 2 mismatches
  • Score 0: no-specific primers
    		•  : Check GGGenome to count off-target sites and carefully use the primers

    Penalty indicates general primer quality. The lower, the better. Details are described at Primer3 manual.

    Primer sequence is linked to GGGenome.
